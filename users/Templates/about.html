About Us

Welcome to CampusHire – the official job portal of Zeal Institute of Business Administration, Computer Application, and Research. Our platform is dedicated to connecting students with their dream careers and helping recruiters find the best talent from our college.

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a robust ecosystem that simplifies the placement process for both students and TPO. We aim to provide a seamless and transparent platform where opportunities meet talent.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We believe in maintaining the highest standards of honesty and ethics.
  • Innovation: Constantly evolving to provide the best experience for our users.
  • Collaboration: Building strong relationships with companies and students for mutual growth.
  • Excellence: Striving to deliver the best services and outcomes.

What We Offer

For Students

  • Access to a wide range of placement opportunities.
  • Personalized dashboards to manage job applications.
  • Resume building and interview preparation resources.
  • Real-time updates on placement activities and notifications.

For Recruiters

  • Efficient job posting and candidate management.
  • Access to a pool of talented students from diverse backgrounds.
  • Direct communication with shortlisted candidates.
  • Customized recruitment solutions for your needs.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a thriving placement environment that bridges the gap between academia and industry. We aim to be the go-to platform for both students and recruiters, ensuring a win-win scenario for everyone involved.

How It Works

  1. Student Registration: Students can create an account, fill out their profiles, and upload their resumes.
  2. Job Listings: TPO can post job opportunities and specify the required qualifications.
  3. Application Process: Students apply for jobs, and TPO can review and shortlist candidates.
  4. Placement Assistance: Our team supports students with interview preparation, resume reviews, and more.

Our Team

CampusHire is managed by a dedicated team of faculty members, student volunteers, and technical experts who ensure the platform runs smoothly and meets the needs of its users. We work tirelessly to provide the best experience and help our students achieve their career goals.
